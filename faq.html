Frequently Asked Questions

A. Full Movie Setup Instructions

Q. How far do you deliver?

A. We deliver rentals to the surrounding areas of Logan, Utah. Check out our pricing map at the bottom of our rentals page for more information. Please note that pricing for the Starlit Cinema package is free inside the Black Radious and has special pricing outside of the Black Radius.

Q. What do I need to have prepared for a Starlit Cinema movie system rental?

A. A 15A circuit outlet and either a DVD or downloaded movie on an electronic device is all you need to have!

Q. Should I rent the Soundboks portable speaker or the Alto sound system?

A. The Soundboks is perfect if there are no available power outlets, as background music for large events, or as a loudspeaker for groups of 150 or less. The Alto sound system is perfect when there are easy access power outlets and when very loud sound is necessary, namely large scale dances, parties, or public address.

Q. How much does chocolate fondue for the Sephra chocolate fountain cost?

A. Depending on the desired quality of chocolate, prices are $90-$130 per 20 pounds of chocolate. 20 pounds of chocolate can reasonably serve 150-300 people. Please reserve the Sephra chocolate fountain at least 2 weeks early to get your desired chocolate in time!
